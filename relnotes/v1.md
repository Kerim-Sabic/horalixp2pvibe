## Horalix P2P 2 is coming

Horalix P2P version 1.x will soon be replaced by Horalix P2P version 2.x.
Version 2 brings a new database format and various cleanups, but remains
protocol compatible with Horalix P2P 1.

More detailed information about Horalix P2P 2 can be found in the release
notes at https://github.com/syncthing/syncthing/releases.

This release is also available as:

* APT repository: https://apt.horalix.com/

* Docker image: `docker.io/kerim-sabic/horalixp2pvibe:{{.version}}` or `ghcr.io/kerim-sabic/horalixp2pvibe:{{.version}}`
  (`{docker,ghcr}.io/syncthing/syncthing:1` to follow just the major version)
